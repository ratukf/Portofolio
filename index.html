About Me


I'm Ratu Kila and I'm a freelance realist artist & illustrator based in Bandung, Indonesia and currently studying in Universitas Padjadajran. I draw pencil portrait, cats using watercolor, landscape in acrylic, and portrait digital paint. I'm an expert in realist pencil portrait art and i have been doing 50+ projects on it. Currently deep diving into watercolor paint technique. I've handled painting project since 2017. See my works on @bvrningmoon
